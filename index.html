What is Bathtub Refinishing?

Bathtub refinishing—also known as bathtub reglazing or bathtub restoration—is a process where a professional applies a new coating to your existing bathtub surface. This not only revives the look of the tub but also extends its life by years.

With services from Bathtub Reglazing NY, you can avoid the high cost and hassle of a full bathtub replacement. Instead, we provide a cost-effective and environmentally friendly alternative that transforms your old bathtub in just a few hours.
